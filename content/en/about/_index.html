Plusserver offers a range of Anything-as-a-Service (XaaS) solutions, including managed databases such as MariaDB and MySQL as a Service. These services allow businesses to easily and quickly set up and manage their databases with flexible and scalable options. Plusserver takes care of routine tasks such as backups, patching, and monitoring, freeing up IT departments and ensuring a smooth and secure operation. The services feature transparent pricing, no minimum contract period, and self-service options. As part of Plusserver's XaaS offerings, MariaDB and MySQL as a Service are hosted in secure, certified German data centers, ensuring data sovereignty and compliance.
